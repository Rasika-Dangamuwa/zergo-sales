Commission Calculation
How Commission is Calculated:
Transaction Types:
  • Bill Created: Initial commission calculated
  • Payment Received: Commission on collected amount
  • Return Processed: Commission reduced
  • Write-off Executed: Commission adjusted
Rate Application:
  • Each transaction uses the rate effective on that date
  • Automatic calculation via Django signals
  • Running balance maintained per sales rep
  • Commission based on bill creator (sales_rep)
